Purpose

This service manages manual Movements in manual warehouses. These kind of tasks are basically used to move inventory between different Locations. Movements are used for replenishment, stocktaking or goods-in operations.

Features

  • Different types of Movements exist: Inbound, Outbound, Replenishment, Reconciliation etc.
  • The type of Movement can be given at creation, or it is trying to be resolved from the Movement attributes
  • Movements can be grouped into groups
